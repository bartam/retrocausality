The Oracle at Delphi

Delphi, Greece — 480 BC

Predicted Greece's victory at Salamis in exact detail — troop counts, weather, the positioning of Persian ships — months before the battle was fought.


Historians attribute it to "intelligence networks."


The Pythia never left the temple.

The Aberfan Premonitions

Wales — 1966

A coal slag landslide buried a school, killing 144. Psychiatrist John Barker documented 76 verified premonitions submitted before the disaster.


Among them — drawings by children who survived, depicting black waves consuming the village.


Barker published the findings. He died the following year.
