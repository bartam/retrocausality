The Physics

Transactional Interpretation

John Cramer, 1986

Quantum events are "handshakes" between waves traveling forward and backward through time.


Every particle interaction is a negotiation across temporal directions.

Time-Symmetric Quantum Mechanics

Aharonov, Bergmann & Lebowitz, 1964

The equations of quantum mechanics work identically forward and backward.


The "arrow of time" is our assumption — not the math's.
